Overview

How To HiTECH DTM is a music production tutorial with a bold and strategic identity. Focused on building a 0 -> 1 channel debut, the video blends creativity with education to teach and inspire musicians as well as grow an online brand. The result was +30k views and +500 digital sales, starting from a channel with 0 views and 0 subscribers.

Production cycle took 3 months. It involved the creation of the song / music project file, Scripting & Storyboarding, and Filming & Editing.
